Campaign Pillar Alignment

Storytelling

Each print is a chapter. The 7 locations map to a narrative arc from isolation to community. The poetic captions are micro-stories. Collecting them assembles a personal urban field guide.

Innovative Technology

Physical-to-digital bridge via unique QR codes. Each physical artifact unlocks a living digital counterpart. No app required — web-based activation keeps the barrier to entry at zero.

Brand Content Integration

L.L.Bean appears as curator, not advertiser. "Field Notes" owns the visual identity. The brand earns presence through the quality of the experience, not the size of the logo.

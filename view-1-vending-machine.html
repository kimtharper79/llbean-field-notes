5 Physical Dimensions ~60" H × 24" W × 18" D (~200 lbs). Floor-bolted mounting for security. Fits in transit shelters, park entrances, coffee shop vestibules. Designed to feel like trail equipment, not a candy machine.
6 NFC Tap-to-Pay Apple Pay, Google Pay, contactless card. $3–5 per print. No cash, no bills — frictionless. Price point matches Gen Z micro-transaction comfort zone (coffee, sticker, transit fare).

Campaign Pillar Alignment

Storytelling

The machine IS a character in the narrative — a mysterious artifact placed along the urban trail. Discovering it is part of the story. The location badge anchors each machine to a chapter.

Innovative Technology

On-demand thermal sublimation printing. NFC contactless payment. Cellular-connected inventory and analytics. Each print has a unique QR code linking physical to digital.

Brand Content Integration

L.L.Bean provides the infrastructure of discovery. The machine's field-equipment aesthetic aligns with the brand's heritage without screaming "sponsored." It curates the experience.

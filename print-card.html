Field Notes — Print Cards (5×7″ Photo Paper)

  1. Load 5×7″ photo paper in your printer
  2. Print this page at 100% scale (uncheck “fit to page”)
  3. Page 1 prints the fronts (photos). Page 2 prints the backs (QR codes).
  4. After printing page 1, flip the paper and feed it back through to print page 2 on the reverse side
  5. Cut along the dashed line between the two cards
  6. Hand to your professor — they scan the QR code with their phone camera to open the demo

Tip: In your printer dialog, set paper size to 5×7″ and select “borderless” if available. Make sure scaling is set to 100% / actual size.

Field Notes lives at the intersection of documentary photography and collectible culture. The physical print is an artifact of place. The digital collectible is proof of presence.

Seven vending machines placed at urban checkpoint locations dispense limited-edition photo prints. Each print's unique QR code unlocks a digital collectible card with documentary story content. Collect all seven to complete the narrative arc — from solitary commute to community gathering.

01

Physical Vending Machine

Exterior design, field equipment locker aesthetic, hardware specs, deployment context. The physical artifact that starts the experience.

 02

Physical Print Design

Front and back face wireframes, 2×3" documentary photo format, QR code bridge, 8-card collection grid. What you hold in your hand.

 03

Digital Activation Flow

5-screen mobile journey: QR scan → loading animation → card reveal → confirmation → gallery. The bridge from physical to digital.

 04

Digital Field Notes Gallery

Personal collection dashboard, card detail with documentary story content, progress tracker, community layer, social sharing. Your growing archive.

 05

Rare / Limited Edition System

Pop-up event machine, animated gold digital cards, set completion unlock, community milestone (500 prints → collaborative card). The reward for dedication.
